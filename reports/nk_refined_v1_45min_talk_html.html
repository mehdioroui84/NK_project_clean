Storyline

One clean reference, not a long list of cluster names

Problem

Original labels and high-resolution clusters were useful for discovery, but too fragmented for a stable classifier target.

Approach

Use full-data latent structure and marker evidence to collapse 25 Leiden 0.4 clusters into 15 refined-v1 labels.

Evaluation

Retrain SCANVI on refined labels and evaluate validation, full-data, zero-shot, and surgery transfer settings.

Decision rule

Keep a label only when it is biologically interpretable and learnable; otherwise collapse into a defensible broader state.

Metric definitions | batch key comparison

Silhouette-based metrics used in the batch-key comparison

Per-cell silhouette

si=bi−aimax(ai,bi)

ai is mean distance to the same label; bi is mean distance to the nearest other label.

NK-state ASW / biology preservation

ASWNK=1+1N∑i=1Nsi(NK_State)2

This matches the implementation: `silhouette_score(..., labels=NK_State)` rescaled from [-1, 1] to [0, 1]. Higher means stronger preservation of NK-state structure.

Dataset/assay ASW mixing, label-aware

MASW,B|Y=∑yYny[1−|1ny∑i∈ysi(B)|]∑yYny

B is either dataset_id or assay_clean. Silhouette is computed within each NK-state label, then transformed as 1 − |mean silhouette|. Higher means better batch mixing within biology.

Metric definitions | kNN and graph metrics

kNN and graph metrics used in the batch-key comparison

kNN label accuracy

AcckNN,Y=1N∑i=1N𝟙[yi=mode({yj:j∈Nk(i)})]

Higher means local neighborhoods preserve NK-state labels.

Within-label kNN batch predictability

AcckNN,B|Y=∑yYnyAcckNN,B(Y=y)∑yYny

Lower raw batch predictability is better. The reported mixing score below rescales this so higher is better.

Normalized kNN batch mixing score

MkNN,B=1−max(0,AcckNN,B|Y−BaseB)1−BaseB

`Base` is the within-label majority-batch baseline. The final score is clipped to [0, 1]. Higher means batch is not more predictable than baseline.

Graph connectivity

GC=1|Y|∑yYmax(connected component size in class y)ny

Higher means cells from the same NK-state label remain connected in the latent kNN graph.

Conclusions

Main conclusions for the refined-v1 reference

  • assay_clean is the production batch key because it preserves NK-state biology while maintaining strong graph connectivity.
  • Leiden 0.4 is a good scaffold, but not the final biological label set.
  • 25 clusters collapse to 15 labels, with B and T retained as broad non-NK teaching classes.
  • Refined SCANVI is strong on validation/full data and gives useful zero-shot transfer.
  • Surgery should not be claimed as an improvement in the current ScaleBio test.
